## 2026 Packaging Trends: The Rise of AI-Powered Small-Batch Customization

### The Old Pain Points

For decades, custom packaging meant:
- **MOQ 500+**: Small brands stuck with inventory they couldn't move
- **3–5 day quotes**: Back-and-forth emails about dimensions, materials, finishes
- **Slow prototyping**: One revision = one week. Trial-and-error was expensive.
- **Over-packaging everywhere**: Mixed materials, non-recyclable, "unbox then trash"

### Why "Packaging Steps Back, Products Step Forward"

Over-packaging is brand insecurity — hiding a mediocre product behind a flashy box.

Great packaging:
- **Protects** — science-based, nothing extra
- **Stays quiet** — clean design, product-first
- **Exits gracefully** — mono-material, fully recyclable
